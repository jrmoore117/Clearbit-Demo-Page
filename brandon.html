Clearbit for Marketo Forms

Clearbit for Marketo Forms allows you to shorten your Marketo forms down to a single field, Email, and hides all other fields, like First and Last Name, Company Name, Industry, Job Title, Phone Number, etc. It uses emails entered into the form to automatically fill in all the hidden fields on the form. If Clearbit cannot fill in one of the hidden form fields, it will automatically show that field to the user so they can fill it in themselves. The benefit of this product is to keep forms as short as possible, increasing the likelihood that a site visitor will convert and submit the form.
