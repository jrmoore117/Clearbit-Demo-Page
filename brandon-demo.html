Clearbit Reveal

Clearbit Reveal is JavaScript that you add to your website to identify which companies are visiting and what pages they are viewing. Once a company is identified by the Clearbit script, they are added to your account in the Clearbit Platform. Once you have identified a bunch of companies, the Clearbit platform allows you to configure powerful features, such as Sales Alerts, Advertising Audiences, and more.

You can see what companies have been identified on your website by logging into your Clearbit Platform account.

Clearbit Forms

Clearbit Forms is JavaScript that you add to your website that allows you to shorten your Marketo, HubSpot, or Pardot forms to a single field, Email, and hides all other fields, like First and Last Name, Company Name, Industry, Job Title, Phone Number, etc.

It uses emails entered into the form to automatically fill in all the hidden fields on the form. If Clearbit cannot fill in one of the hidden form fields, it will automatically show that field to the user so they can fill it in themselves. The benefit of this product is to keep forms as short as possible, increasing the likelihood that a site visitor will convert and submit the form.
